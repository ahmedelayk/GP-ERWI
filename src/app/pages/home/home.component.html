About Erwy System

Smart irrigation is a method of using science and technology to save water in irrigation. It contains weather sensors, soil sensors and various controllers (Web application And Mobile app). The sensor monitors the current weather conditions and the actual ground humidity, and the controller(Mobile app) controls the water valve to open or close.

HOW DO SMARTIRRIGATIONSYSTEMS WORK?

Smart irrigation systems monitor weather, soil moisture, evaporation to automatically adjust watering schedules, avoiding the waste of water caused by traditional preset hat irrigation.The managers can remotely control it through a mobile Application OR web Application. No need for the irrigator to manually open or close the water value
